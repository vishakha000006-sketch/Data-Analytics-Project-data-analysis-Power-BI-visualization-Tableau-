Key Insights
📈
Q4 Drives 38% of Annual Revenue
November and December alone account for $1.83M. Seasonal promotions show a 2.4× uplift over baseline — recommend increasing ad spend by 30% in Oct pre-season.
🎯
Champions Segment Underserved
Top 8% of customers contribute 41% of revenue, yet receive identical comms as low-value segments. A dedicated VIP retention programme could reduce churn by an estimated 4–6pp.
⚠️
Electronics Margin Compression
Electronics revenue grew 24% YoY but gross margin fell from 38% → 29%. Discounting to drive volume is eroding profit. Recommend a price-floor policy for high-velocity SKUs.
